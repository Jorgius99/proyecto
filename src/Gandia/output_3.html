Museu Faller (Gandia)

View on OCity map

The Museu Faller of Gandia shows from a modern perspective the tradition of a centennial celebration, the Fallas, declared Intangible Heritage of Humanity by Unesco. This building has become a benchmark in the interpretation of the culture and identity of the traditional Valencian festival. It shows everything related to the Fallas in general and, also, the connection of these with the city: the process of creating a Falla, the "Ninots" and the job of fallero artist, unique in the worl. Using multimedia resources, audio and video materials, people can enter in an immersive environment to feel the vibrant emotions of fireworks, music and fire.
