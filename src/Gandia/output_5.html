Infraestructura del agua en Palma de Gandia (Gandia)

View on OCity map

The origin of irrigation in the Safor region is due to the Muslim presence, which led to the consolidation, with the creation of new techniques and irrigation elements. They introduced new elements in the organization and regulation of irrigation, where the creation of the cequiero that they endowed, together with the community of irrigators, with a tribal structure, unlike the Christian one, which will be of a union nature, stands out, and in the innovation of new crop species . Another of the additions is the flow division unit, the row, this will be the unit used until the 20th century, some think that it is a fixed amount of water and others as an amount of water proportional to the amount of water at that time. channel. They provided a legal body for the regulation and distribution of flows. The canal system was consolidated with the construction of canals, partidores, dams and other irrigation elements. Among the infrastructures, we can highlight the assut de Palma or the Vernissa, which is in charge of diverting the waters along the left bank, causing the drought of the Vernissa, which has allowed the irrigation of the left bank of the Vernissa and Serpís, made of mortar and concrete , although in certain places primitive stone and mortar material are observed. There are also several aqueducts or arches, which served to save the river from the drought, the channel is made of calicanto and the aqueduct is made of masonry and mortar.
