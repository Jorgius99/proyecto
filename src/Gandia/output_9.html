Playa Norte de Gandía (Gandia)

View on OCity map

Gandia has one of the longest and most extensive beaches with almost 7 km of fine sand. The coastline divides this area into 4 beaches: Els Marenys de Raflacaid, Venice beach, Nord beach and l’Ahuir beach. The most touristic and important beach is the Nord beach, which has all the safety, quality and environmental certifications. In the 1960s, with the national tourism boom, Gandia beach had a rapid development: the construction of hotels, apartments and the promenade began. It is currently one of the main tourist destinations in the Mediterranean due to its quality of the sand and the waters, certified by the blue flag, a European quality seal that it has received for the last 34 years in a row. It offers all kinds of water activities in the yacht club: sailing, rowing, windsurfing, etc., as well as cultural activities on the seafront during the summer, volleyball sports competitions and all kinds of daytime and nightlife.
